About Me

The Story Behind the Lens

Hi, I'm Arun Kumar, and I am the founder of Flawless Photography, which I started in 2021 with a passion for capturing beautiful and meaningful moments.

Photography is more than just a profession for me – it is a way to preserve emotions, stories, and memories that last a lifetime.

Every event is unique, and my goal is to capture genuine smiles, heartfelt moments, and the little details that make each celebration unforgettable.

I specialize in Wedding Photography, Engagement Shoots, Pre-Wedding Sessions, Maternity Photography, and Birthday Events. With a creative approach and attention to detail, I aim to deliver images that are elegant, emotional, and memorable.

Along with me, Flawless Photography also has a dedicated and creative team that works together to capture every moment perfectly and deliver a professional and flawless experience for every client.
